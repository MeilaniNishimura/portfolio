My Story

A little bit about my background.

My name is Meilani Nishimura. I was born in Honolulu, Hawaii in 1998, the youngest of four girls. Unfortunately, I left Hawaii at a young age. I spent my childhood in Orlando, Florida, then moved to the great state of Texas. I went to high school in Alabama, and came back to Texas 6 years ago.

Present Day

More about me now.

I have most recently been working in the healthcare industry as a phlebotomist. I have always been interested in computer science and coding, but didn't know how to go about learning to code. When I discovered coding temple, I knew this would be a great place for me to begin. My partner and I are currently expecting our first child, and she has motivated me to finally enter the coding world.

I hope to build a solid foundation for a future career here at coding temple. My daughter is due to arrive four days after the end of the cohort, and I hope to make her proud!
